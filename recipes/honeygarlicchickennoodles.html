Crockpot Honey Garlic Chicken and Noodles

Summary

Honey garlic chicken and noodles is a delightful dish that combines tender chicken, savory garlic, and sweet honey, served over a bed of flavorful noodles. This popular Asian-inspired recipe offers a harmonious blend of contrasting flavors and textures. The chicken is typically marinated in a mixture of honey, garlic, soy sauce, and other seasonings, then pan-fried or grilled until cooked through and caramelized. The resulting chicken is succulent and coated with a sticky, aromatic glaze. Served alongside a generous portion of noodles, which can be stir-fried with vegetables for added color and crunch, this dish offers a satisfying meal that satisfies both sweet and savory cravings.

Ingedients

  • 400g chicken thighs
  • 3 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 7 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp cornflour
  • 1 tbsp garlic
  • 4 tbsp siracha
  • 100 ml chicken broth
  • 200g noodles
  • 1 tbsp hoison
  • 1 tbsp ground ginger

Steps

  1. Add everything to the crockpot for 4-5 hours on high, or 7-8 hours on low. Stir chicken to break it apart.
  2. Cook your choice of noodles and add to crockpot
  3. Enjoy!
